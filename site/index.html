About the Project

About SiteSpy

SiteSpy is a mobile-first construction project records and cost estimation app built with Expo React Native, Firebase, and Firestore. It helps construction teams capture measurements, estimate material costs, and review project history on any device — even in low-connectivity field conditions.

React Native Expo Firebase Firestore
My Contribution

My Contribution

I focused on how SiteSpy presents financial data to users after a project estimate is completed. My work covers the BOQ report summary, cost breakdown cards, and the estimate result screens that turn raw measurements into readable cost outputs. I reviewed and tested the reportService.js pipeline and both EstimateResultCard.js and CostBreakdownCard.js to ensure the numbers are accurate, clearly formatted, and easy to read on a mobile screen.

My Reflection

Working on the reporting pipeline showed me how important clear output is in construction software. Users need to trust what they see — a wrong total or a cluttered card loses that trust instantly. I learned to balance technical accuracy with clean, readable UI presentation.
